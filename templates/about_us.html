Who are we?

NOTE: Our products are so good, even your plumber will be jealous!

We’re Nepal’s premier bathroom fittings supplier. From luxurious faucets to stylish basins, we’ve got everything you need to make your bathroom stand out! Whether you're looking to impress your guests or just want to feel like royalty every time you brush your teeth, we've got you covered.

Our journey began in the heart of Chitwan, Nepal, where we recognized the demand for premium bathroom fittings that blend functionality with style. Since 2000 AD, we have earned a reputation for delivering top-notch products and exceptional customer service. We source the finest materials and collaborate with renowned brands such as Topsan for bathroom fittings, and Pushti and Venus for gate valves and ball valves, ensuring our customers receive the best value for their investment.

Our dedicated team is always ready to assist you in finding the perfect fittings, ensuring every detail is meticulously attended to. Join us in our mission to transform every bathroom in Nepal into a sanctuary of elegance and comfort.
